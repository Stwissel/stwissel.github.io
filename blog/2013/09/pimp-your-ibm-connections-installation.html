Pimp your IBM Connections installation

Depending how you look at it, IBM Connections is something different:
  1. A leading Social Business application, ready to use
  2. A set of services and APIs to integrate into your applications
  3. A platform to build social enabled applications
( Like Steve Jobs: a touch enabled iPod, an enhanced phone and an Internet access device - 3 items in one device). There are a growing number of applications and add-ons available that take advantage of item two and three. Markus Thömmes compiled a nice list in an (for now) internal presentation. I'm listing the mentioned products in short (in no specific order) here:
  • Trilog Group - ProjExec®: Project Management (Trilog is the organisation where XPages has its roots as JEE technology called Flowbuilder)
  • SugarCRM: Customer Relationship Management. IBM switched from Siebel to SugarCRM recently
  • Immersive for Atlassian: Integrate Confluence and Jira into Connections
  • Hype: Campaign based ideation
  • dynamicSignal: Coupons, promotions and job postings
  • OnTime® Social: Powerful team scheduling powered by Mikkel Flindt Heisterberg
  • MindJet: MindMaps feed and are fed by Connections data
  • VSee: Video conferencing and screen share
  • HarQen Hypervoice™: Voice conversation integration
  • The Hive: motivation and awards
  • Kudos Badges: Gamification and motivation
  • KangoGift: Social recognition
  • Bunchball Nitro: accellerated onboarding, leaderboards
  • Lialis WebDAV: access files through standard file managers
  • filedog Q: local sync for files
  • Box: Intregration into box.com
  • Genus Media Upshot: Rich Media Content Management
  • Cobra Image Router: seamless integration of paper content. Cobra is around for a long time and probably are one of the most experienced providers of transfer from paper to electronic workflows
  • The Poll: Polls and results
  • Questionmine™: video surveys, quizzes, and automated webinars
  • JustNudge Widgets: Widget collection to improve user experience and enable extra tasks
  • EBF.CUSTOMIZER: Customize Connections UI
  • SnappLinks™: Enhancement for the CK Editor for link management
  • Ephox EditLive!: Advanced Editor for Connections
  • LinguaSys – TGSocial: Translation plugin with support for over 110 languages
  • Actiance Vantage: Compliance for SEC, FINRA, IIROC, PRA and FCA
  • Connections Administration Toolkit: If you are not a fan of Jython and the command line, that's the UI you want to have
The list is growing, keep updated on Greenhouse
